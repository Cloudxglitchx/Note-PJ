Learn Together, Smarter

This isn’t just note-taking — it’s a classroom community. Teachers share lesson overviews, students upload their notes, and everyone can upvote, highlight, and ask questions. Our AI adds an extra boost with quick summaries, smart study tips, and even quiz questions to help you prepare. Whether you’re reviewing today’s class or discovering top notes from friends, it’s the easiest way to learn, share, and stay connected.

Who it’s for

  • Students: Upload, share, and rate notes; highlight important points; ask questions; and explore resources shared by classmates.
  • Teachers/Admins: Post daily lesson overviews, guide discussions, moderate content, and track class engagement.

What makes it powerful

  • Organize Your Notes: Upload PDFs, images, or text with optional titles, tags, and timestamps.
  • Discover the Best Content: Upvote notes, see daily top-rated posts, and filter by tags, authors, or keywords.
  • Engage with Classmates: Highlight sections, ask questions directly in notes, and join Q&A discussions.
  • AI-Powered Support: Get instant summaries, suggested tags, study tips, and quiz questions to sharpen your learning.
  • Stay in the Loop: Notifications keep you updated when someone answers your question or interacts with your notes.
  • Personalize Your Experience: Save favorites, explore related resources, and switch between light and dark mode on any device.

By combining student collaboration, teacher guidance, and AI assistance, this app transforms note-taking into an interactive, engaging, and smarter way to learn together.
